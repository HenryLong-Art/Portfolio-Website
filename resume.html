The President is responsible for the wellbeing of each team member, helping to manage four sub-committees, communicating with professors, helping with merch management, organizing a convention which typically has several hundred attendees, and most importantly, continuing to be the biggest cheerleader for the students the Shrunkenheadman club supports.
